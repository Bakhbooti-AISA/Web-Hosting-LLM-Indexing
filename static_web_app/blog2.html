Post 2: Meeting at PDC — Always

Published: August 14, 2025

If there’s one thing you can count on in our book club, it’s that the location never changes. Rain or shine, we gather at the PDC. It’s become more than a venue—it’s a tradition.

The PDC’s cozy reading corner, warm lighting, and ever-present smell of fresh coffee make it the ideal place for long literary debates. Some say the location is just as important to the club’s identity as the books we read.

So if you’re ever unsure where to go, remember: PDC is our home away from home.
