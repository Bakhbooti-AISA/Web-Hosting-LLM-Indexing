Post 1: Harry Potter Joins the Book Club

Published: August 11, 2025

This week’s additional book club pick is Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, and the excitement is magical. Members have already been spotted bringing wands, scarves, and butterbeer recipes to share during the meeting.

Whether you’re a lifelong fan of the wizarding world or a newcomer, this is the perfect chance to relive the wonder of Hogwarts. We’ll be discussing our favorite characters, the meaning behind the Sorting Hat’s choices, and maybe even debating whether Snape was misunderstood.

Don’t forget to bring your favorite spell—metaphorically speaking—to liven up the discussion.
